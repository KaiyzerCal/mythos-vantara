# Pipeline: Deep Research

Pure investigation. Researcher only, deeper scope than the standard deliverable's research step.

## When to Use

- Competitor deep-dives
- Market landscape mapping before a strategy decision
- Background research on a person, company, or concept
- Investigation-only requests where no deliverable is planned yet

## Chain

```
Researcher (deep) → Calvin review → (optionally) Strategist
```

## Steps

### 1. Scope the research

Orchestrator confirms with Calvin:
- What question are we trying to answer?
- What does "complete" look like?
- Is this feeding a specific deliverable or is it standalone?

### 2. Researcher runs deep

More thoroughness than standard research:
- Multiple source types (primary, secondary, original sources if reachable)
- Competitor products: features, pricing, positioning, messaging, review sentiment
- Gap analysis: what does the landscape not address?

### 3. Research brief delivered

File placed in `Projects/[name]/research/` or `Outbox/` if no project exists yet.

### 4. Calvin review

Calvin reviews before anything is acted on. Research is reference — not strategy.

### 5. Optional: Strategist

If the research is feeding a strategy decision, route to Strategist next.

## Rules

- Researcher does not form opinions. They surface findings.
- Every claim attributed.
- Gaps named explicitly — don't imply complete coverage when it isn't.
