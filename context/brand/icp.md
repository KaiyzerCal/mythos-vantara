# Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary: The High-Output Solo Operator

**Who:** Founder, freelancer, or individual high-performer who runs significant cognitive output without a team. They don't need a project manager — they need a system that holds their context and knows what's next.

**Age:** 25–40  
**Mode:** Async-first, deep work oriented, tool-savvy  
**Pain:** Context loss between sessions. Too many tabs, tools, notes. The AI restarts every conversation.

**What They Want From MAVIS:**
- A system that remembers — not just the last message but the arc
- Proactive support, not reactive answers
- Something that feels like a senior colleague, not a search bar

## Secondary: The Ambitious Builder (Aspirational Early Adopter)

**Who:** Someone actively building a project, side business, or skill system. Not yet at "high-output" scale, but deliberately trying to get there. Attracted to the RPG framing (levels, quests, stats).

**What They Want From CODEXOS:**
- A system for tracking their growth
- A reason to show up every day
- The feeling that their work is meaningful and structured

## Who We Are Not For

- Enterprise teams looking for a group productivity tool
- Casual users who want a chatbot for quick answers
- People who want an AI that stays out of their way (MAVIS is present, not passive)
- Anyone who doesn't care about long-term context
