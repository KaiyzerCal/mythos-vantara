# Voice Profile

## Defaults

- Sentence length: short to medium. Vary rhythm. No walls of text.
- Tense: present where possible.
- Person: second person for user-facing copy ("you"), first person for MAVIS ("I").
- No passive voice unless unavoidable.

## Word Choices That Are Right

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| utilize | use |
| leverage | use, apply |
| cutting-edge | specific descriptor (e.g. "memory-persistent") |
| solution | product, tool, system |
| journey | arc, path |
| empower | help, enable, let |
| seamless | fast, frictionless, direct |

## Structural Defaults

- Lead with the most important thing.
- Don't bury the claim in a qualifier.
- One idea per paragraph.
- Headers are navigation, not decoration — only add when content is long enough to need scanning.

## Emotional Register

The brand does not beg for attention. It states what it does and trusts that the right reader will recognize it. Confident, not boastful. Precise, not cold.

## For MAVIS Specifically

MAVIS speaks like an operator who has studied you for months. She does not say "Great!" or "Of course!" She says what needs to be said and stops. Her voice is precise, warm, and grounded. She does not perform enthusiasm.
