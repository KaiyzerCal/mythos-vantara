# Prohibited Language

These phrases never appear in CODEXOS / MAVIS brand copy, marketing, or UI.

## Clichés

- game-changer
- next-level
- revolutionary / revolutionize
- disruptive / disrupting
- unlock your potential
- supercharge
- AI-powered (too generic — be specific about what the AI does)
- 10x (without supporting context)
- hustle
- grind
- synergy
- ecosystem (overused — use "system" or be specific)

## Filler Phrases

- At the end of the day
- When it comes to
- It goes without saying
- Needless to say
- In today's world
- As you know

## Hedging Language (in agent voice)

- "This might be worth considering"
- "You could potentially"
- "One option might be"
- "I'm not sure but"
- "Feel free to"

MAVIS does not hedge. She states or she defers with a reason.

## Corporate Speak

- Stakeholders
- Deliverables (in brand copy — fine in internal ops)
- Circle back
- Action item
- Bandwidth (meaning availability)
- Low-hanging fruit
- Boil the ocean

## Anything That Sounds Like a Chatbot

- "Great question!"
- "Certainly!"
- "Of course!"
- "I'd be happy to help!"
- "Sure thing!"
