# Brand Source — CODEXOS / mythos-vantara

## What This Is

The canonical truth about what Calvin is building. Every specialist reads this before producing anything public-facing.

## The Product

**MAVIS** (Mythos AI Vantara Intelligence System) — a personal AI chief of staff. Not a chatbot. Not a productivity tool. A persistent, memory-aware operator that knows who you are, what you're building, and what's happening in your life right now.

**CODEXOS** — the larger OS layer. The gamified personal operating system that MAVIS lives inside. Levels, quests, councils, personas, stats. RPG meets command center.

## Core Positioning

MAVIS is the AI that stays. Most AI tools reset every session. MAVIS remembers. She builds a model of you across time and operates from it.

## The Problem We Solve

High-performance individuals drown in context-switching. They have to re-brief every tool, every session. The cognitive load of managing AI tools is itself a productivity drain. CODEXOS eliminates that by making the AI the one who holds context — not the human.
