# Plan: Brief

For medium-scope work that needs a Strategist brief before the Writer can proceed.

## When to Use

- A piece of content for a specific audience where messaging matters
- A feature that needs a product brief before implementation
- Anything that will go public and needs strategy before drafting

## Template

```
BRIEF PLAN — [title]
Date: [date]
Project folder: Projects/[name]/

---
CONTEXT
What is this for? [background]
Why now? [why this matters today]

---
AUDIENCE
Primary: [who, specifically]
What do they believe now: [current belief/state]
What we want them to believe/do: [desired change]

---
MESSAGE
The one thing: [the single takeaway]
Supporting points (max 3): [list]

---
TONE
[Reference to voice-profile.md section or custom note]

---
FORMAT
Type: [article / post / email / copy / etc]
Length: [approx]
Surface: [where it publishes]

---
CHAIN
[ ] Researcher
[ ] Strategist
[ ] Writer
[ ] Editor
[ ] Calvin review
[ ] Inbox/

---
DEADLINE
[Date or "no hard deadline"]

---
SUCCESS
[How we know this worked]
```
