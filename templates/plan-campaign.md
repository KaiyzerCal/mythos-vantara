# Plan: Campaign

For full-scale multi-surface work. Multiple deliverables, multiple specialists, extended timeline.

## When to Use

- A product launch
- A content campaign across 3+ surfaces
- A major positioning shift that affects multiple pieces simultaneously

## Template

```
CAMPAIGN PLAN — [campaign name]
Date: [date]
Project folder: Projects/[name]/

---
OBJECTIVE
[One sentence: what this campaign achieves]

---
AUDIENCE
Primary ICP: [reference icp.md section or custom]
Key insight: [what do they believe that this campaign addresses]

---
CORE MESSAGE
The through-line: [the single idea that connects all deliverables]
Proof points: [3 max; each must be defensible]

---
DELIVERABLES
1. [Type] — [surface] — [who produces] — [deadline]
2. [Type] — [surface] — [who produces] — [deadline]
3. [Type] — [surface] — [who produces] — [deadline]
...

---
SEQUENCING
[Which deliverable leads? Which amplifies? Which converts?]
Week 1: ...
Week 2: ...
...

---
CHAIN PER DELIVERABLE
Each deliverable runs its own pipeline (see pipeline-deliverable.md).
Track status in Projects/[name]/brief/:
[ ] Deliverable 1 — status
[ ] Deliverable 2 — status
...

---
DEPENDENCIES
[What must exist before X can start]

---
MEASUREMENT
Primary KPI: [one number that tells us if this worked]
Secondary metrics: [supporting signals]
Reporting: [Analyst produces report at end of campaign]

---
GO / NO-GO
[What would cause us to pause or kill this campaign]
```
